DAY 6

Day 6 is Leg Day – focus on building strength, power, and endurance in your lower body.

Every squat, deadlift, and lunge challenges your limits and strengthens your foundation.

Push through fatigue, because growth happens when you embrace the effort.

Strong legs support every movement and elevate your overall performance.

Step, lift, and drive yourself toward a stronger, more resilient you.

Day 6: Legs Workout

Exercise Sets Reps
Deadlifts 5 6-8
Smith Machine Squats 3 6-10
Leg Extension 2 To Failure
Single Leg RDL with Dumbbell 3 8-12
Adductor/Abductor Machine 3 15
Standing Calf Raises 4 15-20
Leg Raises 3 25

Day 6 Diet Plan (Legs Day)

  • Breakfast (Pre-Workout if morning):
    • 4–5 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
    • ½ cup oats with milk + 1 banana
    • 5–6 almonds / walnuts
  • Mid-Morning Snack:
    • 1 scoop whey protein or paneer
    • 1 apple / orange / seasonal fruit
  • Lunch:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / tofu
    • 1–2 cups rice or 2–3 rotis (whole wheat)
    • Steamed vegetables + salad
  • Evening Snack (Pre-Workout if evening):
    • 1 medium sweet potato / 2 bananas
    • 1 boiled egg / peanut butter toast
  • Post-Workout:
    • Whey protein shake + 2 dates or banana
  • Dinner:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / soy chunks
    • 1–2 rotis or small portion of rice
    • Stir-fried veggies + salad
  • Before Bed:
    • 200g Greek yogurt / cottage cheese
    • A few soaked almonds (optional)
