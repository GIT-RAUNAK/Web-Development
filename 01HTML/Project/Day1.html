Day 1: Push Workout (Chest, Shoulders, Biceps)

Exercise Sets Reps
Incline Smith Machine Press / Incline Dumbbell Press 4 6-10
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 2 6-10
Weighted Push-ups / Seated Cable Chest Press 2 To Failure (10-12)
Pec Deck Fly / Cable Fly (Lower Chest Focus) 2 To Failure
Cable Lateral Raises 3 15-20
Straight Bar Bicep Curls (Superset with Cable Crunches) 4 10-15 (Curls) / 25-30 (Crunches)

Day 1 Diet Plan (Push Day)

  • Breakfast (Pre-Workout if morning training):
    • 4-5 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
    • 2 whole wheat bread slices OR oats (½ cup)
    • 1 banana / apple (quick carbs for energy)
  • Mid-Morning Snack:
    • A protein shake (whey or plant-based)
    • A handful of almonds or walnuts
  • Lunch:
    • Grilled chicken breast / paneer / tofu
    • 1-2 cups cooked rice OR quinoa OR roti (whole wheat)
    • Steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach, beans, carrots)
    • Salad with olive oil or lemon dressing
  • Evening Snack (Pre-workout if evening training):
    • 1 sweet potato OR 2 bananas (fast-digesting carbs)
    • Peanut butter toast OR boiled eggs
  • Post-Workout:
    • Whey protein shake (within 30–45 min)
    • 1 banana or 2 dates (for glycogen refill)
  • Dinner:
    • Grilled fish / chicken / paneer / soy chunks
    • Stir-fried or steamed vegetables
    • 1-2 rotis OR small portion of rice
    • Optional: a small bowl of curd / Greek yogurt
  • Before Bed:
    • A glass of warm milk OR 100g Greek yogurt / cottage cheese (slow-digesting protein)
