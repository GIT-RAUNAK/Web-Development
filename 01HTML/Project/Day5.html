DAY 5

Day 5 is Pull Day – strengthen your back, triceps, and rear delts to build power and stability.

Every pull pushes your limits and improves control, posture, and resilience.

Focus on form, engage each muscle, and trust the process for long-term gains.

Embrace the challenge, because growth comes when you step outside your comfort zone.

Pull with intensity, and let every rep bring you closer to your best self.

Day 5: Pull Workout

Exercise Sets Reps

Chest-Supported Close-Grip Row 3 6-10

Day 5 Diet Plan (Pull Day)

  • Breakfast (Pre-Workout if morning):
    • 4–5 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
    • ½ cup oats with milk + 1 banana
    • 5–6 almonds / walnuts
  • Mid-Morning Snack:
    • 1 scoop whey protein or paneer
    • 1 apple / orange / seasonal fruit
  • Lunch:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / tofu
    • 1–2 cups rice or 2–3 rotis (whole wheat)
    • Steamed vegetables + salad
  • Evening Snack (Pre-Workout if evening):
    • 1 medium sweet potato / 2 bananas
    • 1 boiled egg / peanut butter toast
  • Post-Workout:
    • Whey protein shake + 2 dates or banana
  • Dinner:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / soy chunks
    • 1–2 rotis or small portion of rice
    • Stir-fried veggies + salad
  • Before Bed:
    • 200g Greek yogurt / cottage cheese
    • A few soaked almonds (optional)
