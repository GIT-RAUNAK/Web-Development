DAY 2

Day 2 is Pull Day – time to strengthen your back, triceps, and rear delts.

Every pull challenges your limits and builds resilience.

Focus, form, and consistency turn effort into progress.

Embrace the burn, because growth happens outside your comfort zone.

Pull with purpose, and let every rep push you closer to your goals.

Day 2: Pull Workout

Exercise Sets Reps
Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown 4 6-10

Day 2 Diet Plan (Pull Day)

  • Breakfast (Pre-Workout if morning training):
    • 4-5 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
    • ½ cup oats with milk + 1 banana
    • 5–6 almonds / walnuts
  • Mid-Morning Snack:
    • 1 scoop whey protein (or paneer for vegetarian option)
    • 1 apple / orange / seasonal fruit
  • Lunch:
    • 150–200g chicken breast / fish / paneer / tofu
    • 1–2 cups rice or 2–3 rotis (whole wheat)
    • Steamed veggies (spinach, beans, broccoli, carrots)
    • Salad (cucumber, tomato, lettuce)
  • Evening Snack (PreSalad wit-workout if evening training):
    • 1 medium sweet potato / 2 bananas (fast carbs)
    • 1 boiled egg / peanut butter toast
  • Post-Workout:
    • Whey protein shake + 2 dates or banana (for glycogen recovery)
  • Dinner:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / soy chunks
    • 1-2 rotis OR small portion of rice
    • Stir-fried veggies + salad
  • Before Bed:
    • 200g Greek yogurt / cottage cheese (slow protein)
    • A few soaked almonds (optional)
