DAY 3

Day 3 is all about building lower body strength, power, and stability.

Every squat, deadlift, and lunge shapes not just your legs, but your determination.

Push through the burn, because true growth lies in hard work.

Strong legs create a strong foundation – both in the gym and in life.

Embrace every rep, and step closer to your ultimate potential.

Day 3: Legs Workout

Exercise Sets Reps
Squats 4 8-12
Romanian Deadlifts (Barbell) 3 8-12
Hip Thrust 2 15
Leg Extension 2 To Failure
Leg Curl 2 To Failure

Leg Raises 3 25

Day 3 Diet Plan (Legs Day)

  • Breakfast (Pre-Workout if morning training):
    • 4–5 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
    • ½ cup oats with milk + 1 banana
    • 5–6 almonds / walnuts
  • Mid-Morning Snack:
    • 1 scoop whey protein or paneer
    • 1 apple / orange / seasonal fruit
  • Lunch:
    • 150–200g chicken breast / fish / paneer / tofu
    • 1–2 cups rice or 2–3 rotis (whole wheat)
    • Steamed vegetables (spinach, beans, broccoli, carrots)
    • Salad (cucumber, tomato, lettuce)
  • Evening Snack (Pre-Workout if evening training):
    • 1 medium sweet potato / 2 bananas (fast carbs)
    • 1 boiled egg / peanut butter toast
  • Post-Workout:
    • Whey protein shake + 2 dates or banana
  • Dinner:
    • 150–200g chicken / fish / paneer / soy chunks
    • 1–2 rotis or small portion of rice
    • Stir-fried veggies + salad
  • Before Bed:
    • 200g Greek yogurt / cottage cheese (slow protein)
    • A few soaked almonds (optional)
